# Pig Pageant (Drecksau: Sauschön) Rules

## Overview

Pig Pageant is an expansion for Dirty Pig (Drecksau) by Frank Bebenroth, published by Kosmos. It adds a new victory path: instead of only winning with all dirty pigs, you can now also win by making all your pigs into glamorous pageant contestants! **Requires the Dirty Pig base game.**

## New Components

- 16 Pretty Pig (Schönsau) cards
- 12 Escape (Aus dem Staub) cards
- 4 Lucky Bird (Glücksvogel) cards
- 3 Mud Bucket markers + 3 Trophy markers (for tournament scoring)

## Setup

Shuffle all expansion cards into the base game deck. Each player starts with 3 pigs (clean side up) and 3 hand cards, same as the base game.

## New Win Condition

You win by being the first to have ALL your pigs in the **same state**:
- **All Dirty Pigs** — win the Mud Bucket
- **All Pretty Pigs** — win the Golden Trophy

## New Card Effects

### Pretty Pig (Schönsau)
Place on **any** pig card (yours or opponent's), whether clean or dirty. The pig becomes a Pretty Pig.

**Special protections:**
- A Pretty Pig has an **umbrella** — it stays pretty even in the Rain (Rain cards have no effect)
- A barn with a Pretty Pig **cannot be nailed shut** (Farmer's Revenge cannot be attached)
- The "Farmer Scrubs" card **cannot remove** a Pretty Pig

**Restriction:** Cannot be placed on a dirty pig that is in a locked barn (dirty + barn + nailed shut). That pig is the "happiest dirty pig" and must stay dirty for the rest of the game.

### Escape (Aus dem Staub)
Play this card together with a Pretty Pig card to **remove** the Pretty Pig status. The card underneath returns to its previous state (clean or dirty). Can be used on your own or an opponent's Pretty Pig.

This is the **only way** to remove a Pretty Pig card.

### Lucky Bird (Glücksvogel)
Play this card along with **all your other hand cards** (play them all at once for their effects). Then draw **3 new cards** from the draw pile.

## Strategy Tips

- The game is now much more dynamic — your strategy might change mid-game
- Watch your opponents' pigs: if they're close to all-dirty, consider making one of theirs pretty
- The Lucky Bird is very powerful — save it for a big combo turn
