# Dirty Pig (Drecksau) Rules

## Overview

Dirty Pig (originally Drecksau) is a quick and fun card game designed by Frank Bebenroth and published by KOSMOS. Each player has a row of clean pigs, and the goal is simple: be the first player to make ALL of your pigs dirty! Play mud cards, protect your pigs with barns, and use rain to wash your opponents' pigs clean.

## Components

- Pig cards (double-sided: clean on one side, dirty on the other)
- Mud cards
- Rain cards
- Barn cards
- Lightning cards
- Lightning Rod cards
- Farmer cards (scrubs pigs clean)
- Barn Door cards (locks the barn)

## Setup

1. Each player receives pig cards based on player count:
   - **2 players**: 5 pigs each
   - **3 players**: 4 pigs each
   - **4 players**: 3 pigs each
2. Place all pig cards **clean side up** in front of each player.
3. Shuffle all remaining cards and deal **3 cards** to each player.
4. Place the rest face down as the draw pile.

## How to Play

On your turn, you **must play 1 card** from your hand, then **draw 1 card** from the draw pile.

### Card Effects

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Mud** | Flip one of your clean pigs to the **dirty side** |
| **Rain** | ALL unprotected dirty pigs (all players) are washed **clean** |
| **Barn** | Place on one of your pigs. Protects that pig from Rain |
| **Lightning** | Destroy one opponent's Barn (remove it from the game) |
| **Lightning Rod** | Attach to one of your Barns. Protects it from Lightning permanently |
| **Farmer** | The farmer scrubs one dirty pig clean — even pigs inside a Barn! |
| **Barn Door** | Attach to your Barn (must already contain a dirty pig). Protects against the Farmer |

### Special Rules

- If you **cannot play** any of your 3 cards, reveal your hand to all players, discard all 3 cards, and draw 3 new cards.
- A pig in a Barn with a Door and a Lightning Rod is **fully protected** from all threats.
- Cards played on opponents' pigs (Rain, Lightning, Farmer) are discarded after use.
- When the draw pile is empty, shuffle the discard pile to form a new draw pile.

## Winning

The **first player** to have ALL of their pigs showing the dirty side wins immediately!
