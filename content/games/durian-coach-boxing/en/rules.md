# Durian Coach Boxing: Haw! Rules

## Overview

*Durian Coach Boxing: Haw!* is a Monopoly-style board game for 2-4 players, lasting 30-60 minutes. Players take on the role of "Little Know-Nothings" in the world of the "Big Boxing Gym," helping Coach Durian manage the gym by adding various facilities! Don't forget to keep up your daily training! The first player to own **10 houses** becomes the new coach and wins the game!

## Components

- 4 Pawns
- 2 Wooden dice
- 40 Houses (4 colors, 10 each)
- 7 Character cards
- 4 Large map tiles
- 20 Small map tiles
- 80 Game banknotes
- 1 Rulebook

## Setup

### 1. Map Layout

Place the 4 large map tiles at the 4 corners. Arrange small map tiles in rows of 5 between each pair of large tiles. Order doesn't matter — arrange them however you like!

### 2. Player Setup

The player who most recently ate durian goes first! All players proceed clockwise:

1. Choose a character card
2. Choose a matching colored pawn
3. Receive **¥1000** starting bonus
4. Place your pawn at the Start space

## Turn Sequence

Each turn consists of:

1. **Roll dice** — Roll one die and move that many spaces **counter-clockwise**
2. **Trigger effect** — Activate the space's effect (only one space effect per turn)

## Win Condition

The first player to own **10 houses** wins the game.

## Public Spaces

The four corner (large tile) spaces:

### Start

Hard day's work! **Passing** Start: collect **¥200** bonus. **Landing exactly** on Start: collect **¥400** bonus!

### Shower Room

A nice fragrant shower room — no effect.

### Forgot to Book Class

You forgot to book your class! Stand outside the shower as punishment! **Move backward** to the Shower Room.

### Slacking Off

Caught slacking! **Rest** for one turn (skip your next turn).

## Facilities

Any space with a price tag is a "facility." Pay Coach Durian the listed price to purchase or upgrade a facility, then place one of your houses on it.

### Training Facilities

Spaces marked with a level number are "training facilities," organized in color groups:

| Training Facility | Buy/Upgrade Cost |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| Static Stretching | ¥60 |
| Ladder Climb | ¥150 |
| Medicine Ball | ¥250 |
| Target Practice | ¥350 |

#### Upgrading

After the initial purchase, landing on your own training facility again lets you upgrade it by paying the **same price**:

- Upgraded facilities charge higher training fees
- Place an additional house on the facility for each upgrade

#### Double Fees

When you own **all training facilities of the same color**, all training fees for that color are **doubled**!

### Metro Lines

Includes: Line 2, Line 4, Line 8

Fees scale based on how many metro lines you own:

| Metro Lines Owned | Fee |
|-------------------|-----|
| 1 | ¥50 |
| 2 | ¥100 |
| 3 | ¥200 |

### Entertainment Facilities

When another player lands on your entertainment facility, roll **two dice** and they pay **sum of dice × ¥10** as an entertainment fee!

## Selling Facilities

Players may sell their facilities **at any time**:

- **Sell to Coach Durian**: Sell at **50%** of total facility value (including upgrade costs)
- **Auction to other players**: Players take turns bidding; sell to the highest bidder

> Facilities can only be sold as a whole — you cannot sell individual houses separately.

## Bankruptcy

If a player has no facilities, no cash, and still owes debts, that player is declared **bankrupt** and eliminated. Coach Durian reclaims their pawn and remaining houses; other players continue.

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Rest | Skip this turn |
| Penalty | Rest, moving backward, or unfavorable Know-Nothing events |
| Move Backward | Move clockwise on the map; cannot collect Start bonus or trigger any space effects while moving backward |
| Facility | Collective term for training facilities, entertainment facilities, and metro lines |
| Check | Any dice roll other than the movement roll |
| House | Colored wooden blocks placed on facilities when purchased or upgraded |

## Detailed Rules

- **What if you roll double 6 on a Know-Nothing skill?** — Choose any two skills to acquire, or pick one upgraded skill
- **Do backward moves or position swaps trigger space effects?** — No! Only one space effect per turn, and only movement from rolling dice triggers effects
- **Can Xiao Wang collect running-away fees when moving backward?** — No, Xiao Wang can only collect running-away fees when moving **forward**; backward moves and other position changes don't count
- **What about ¥5 amounts?** — Round up to ¥10! All ¥5 values in the game are rounded up
