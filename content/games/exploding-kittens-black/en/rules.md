# Exploding Kittens: Black Box (NSFW Edition) Rules

## Overview

The Black Box edition (officially "NSFW Edition") of Exploding Kittens is the adult-only version of the game. **The rules are identical to the Red Box (original edition)** — only the card artwork and flavor text have been replaced with adult-themed, provocative, and absurdist illustrations.

This is a standalone game for **2-5 players** (does not require the original Red Box). It can also be combined with the Red Box deck to support up to **9 players**.

## Quick Rules Recap

1. **Setup:** Remove all Exploding Kitten and Defuse cards. Deal 1 Defuse to each player + 7 random cards. Shuffle remaining Defuse cards and Exploding Kittens (one fewer than the number of players) back into the draw pile.

2. **On your turn:** Play as many cards as you want (or none), then **draw one card** to end your turn.

3. **If you draw an Exploding Kitten:** You must play a Defuse card or you're eliminated. If you defuse it, secretly place the Exploding Kitten back anywhere in the draw pile.

4. **Last player standing wins.**

## Card Types

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Defuse** | Saves you from an Exploding Kitten |
| **Attack** | End your turn without drawing; next player takes 2 turns |
| **Skip** | End your turn without drawing a card |
| **Favor** | Force another player to give you a card of their choice |
| **Shuffle** | Shuffle the draw pile |
| **See the Future** | Privately view the top 3 cards of the draw pile |
| **Nope** | Cancel any action card played by another player (except Defuse and Exploding Kitten) |
| **Cat Cards** | Collect matching pairs/triples to steal cards from opponents |
