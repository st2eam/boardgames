# Exploding Kittens Rules

## Overview

Exploding Kittens is a highly-strategic, kitty-powered version of Russian Roulette. Players draw cards until someone draws an Exploding Kitten, at which point they explode and are out of the game — unless they have a Defuse card. The last player standing wins.

## Components

- **56 cards** including:
  - 4 Exploding Kittens
  - 6 Defuse cards
  - Various action cards (Attack, Skip, See the Future, etc.)
  - Nope cards
  - Cat cards (used for combos)

## Setup

1. Remove all Exploding Kitten cards (4) and Defuse cards (6) from the deck.
2. Deal 1 Defuse card to each player.
3. Shuffle the remaining Defuse cards back into the deck.
4. Deal 7 cards to each player.
5. Insert a number of Exploding Kittens back into the deck equal to the number of players minus 1. (e.g., 3 players → 2 Exploding Kittens).
6. Remove remaining Exploding Kittens from the game.
7. Shuffle the deck and place it face down.

## How to Play

On your turn, you may play any number of cards (or none), then you **must draw** a card from the top of the deck.

If you draw an Exploding Kitten:
- You are **dead** and out of the game
- UNLESS you have a **Defuse** card — play it to place the Exploding Kitten back into the deck anywhere you want (even on top)

## Card Reference

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Exploding Kitten** | You must defuse it or die |
| **Defuse** | Survive an Exploding Kitten. Put it back in the deck secretly |
| **Attack** | End your turn without drawing. The next player must take 2 turns |
| **Skip** | End your turn without drawing |
| **See the Future** | Look at the top 3 cards of the deck |
| **Shuffle** | Shuffle the deck |
| **Favor** | Force another player to give you 1 card of their choice |
| **Nope** | Cancel any action card (even another Nope). Can be played at any time |
| **Cat Cards** (5 types) | Play as a pair to steal a random card from any player. Play as 3-of-a-kind to name a card and take it from a player |

## Cat Card Combos

- **Two of a kind** — Steal 1 random card from any player
- **Three of a kind** — Name a specific card and take it from any player (if they have it)
- **Five different** — Take any 1 card from the discard pile

## Strategy Tips

- Hold onto Defuse cards — they are your only protection
- Use See the Future before drawing when the deck is small
- Nope cards can save you at critical moments
- Save Cat Cards for combos rather than playing them alone

## Winning

The last player who hasn't exploded wins.
