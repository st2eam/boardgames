# Sanguosha: Disloyal Minister Rules

## Overview

Disloyal Minister (不臣之君) is a 2026 expansion for Sanguosha (Legends of the Three Kingdoms) that revolutionizes the classic identity game mode. Instead of new hero characters, it introduces **7 brand-new identity modes** that fundamentally change how the game's faction dynamics work. Supports 4-8 players. **Requires the Sanguosha base game.**

## Components

- Identity mode cards for 7 new game modes
- Exclusive heroes: Liu Hong (刘宏), Dong Zhuo (董卓), Zhang Jiao (张角)
- Mode-specific role cards and rule cards

## The 7 Identity Modes

This expansion contains 7 distinct identity configurations, each creating a unique social deduction experience. Each mode modifies which roles exist, their win conditions, and how alliances work.

### Key Design Philosophy

Unlike the standard Monarch/Loyalist/Rebel/Spy setup, these modes introduce:
- **Asymmetric information** — some modes give certain players more knowledge
- **Shifting alliances** — some roles can change allegiance mid-game
- **New role types** — including Tyrant (暴君), Madman (失心疯), and others
- **Variable player counts** — each mode specifies its optimal player range

## How to Use

1. Choose one of the 7 identity modes before the game begins.
2. Distribute the mode-specific role cards according to the chosen mode's setup rules.
3. Follow the standard Sanguosha turn structure (Judge → Draw → Play → Discard → End).
4. Win conditions vary by mode — refer to the specific mode's role cards.

## General Rules

- All standard Sanguosha game mechanics remain in effect (basic cards, strategy cards, equipment, hero abilities).
- The expansion's exclusive heroes (Liu Hong, Dong Zhuo, Zhang Jiao) have abilities designed to synergize with the new identity modes.
- Some modes may modify specific rules (e.g., what happens when a player is eliminated).

> **Note:** The detailed rules for each of the 7 identity modes are printed on the included mode cards. Please refer to the physical rulebook and mode cards for the specific setup, roles, and victory conditions of each mode.
