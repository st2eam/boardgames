# Sea Salt & Paper Rules

## Overview

Sea Salt & Paper is a set-collection and push-your-luck card game for 2-4 players, published by Bombyx. Beautifully illustrated with origami-style marine creatures, players collect cards to form scoring combinations, play Duo pairs for special effects, and decide the perfect moment to end each round. The first player to reach the target score wins.

## Components

- 58 playing cards (origami sea creatures in various colors)
- 4 player aid cards
- 2 card distribution reference cards

## Setup

1. Shuffle all 58 playing cards and place them face-down as the draw pile.
2. Reveal the **top 2 cards** and place them face-up next to the deck to form **two discard piles**.
3. Choose the first player randomly.
4. Set the **target score** based on player count:
   - **2 players**: 40 points
   - **3 players**: 35 points
   - **4 players**: 30 points

## How to Play

On your turn, perform these steps in order:

### 1. Take a Card (Mandatory)

Choose **one** of these options:
- **Draw from deck**: Draw 2 cards from the draw pile, keep 1, and place the other face-up on either discard pile (if a discard pile is empty, you must place it there first)
- **Take from discard**: Take the top card from one of the two discard piles

### 2. Play Duo Pairs (Optional)

If you have matching **Duo pairs** in your hand, you may play them face-up in front of you to trigger their effects. You may play multiple Duo pairs in one turn.

| Duo Pair | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **2 Crabs** | Browse one discard pile and take any card from it |
| **2 Boats** | Take another turn immediately |
| **2 Fish** | Draw 1 card from the draw pile |
| **Swimmer + Shark** | Steal 1 random card from another player's hand |

### 3. End the Round (Optional)

If your cards (in hand + in front of you) total **7 or more points**, you may choose to end the round by announcing one of:

**"STOP"** — The round ends immediately. All players reveal their hands and score their card points. No color bonus is awarded.

**"LAST CHANCE"** — Each other player takes one final turn, then all reveal hands.
- If the caller has the **highest or tied score**: They score their card points **plus** a color bonus (1 point per card of their most abundant color). Other players score only their color bonus.
- If the caller **does not** have the highest score: They score only their color bonus. Other players score their full card points.

## Card Types & Scoring

### Collection Cards (scored by quantity)

| Collection | Cards | Points by count |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| **Shells** | 6 cards | 0, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10 |
| **Octopus** | 5 cards | 0, 3, 6, 9, 12 |
| **Penguins** | 3 cards | 1, 3, 5 |
| **Sailors** | 2 cards | 0, 5 |

### Duo Cards (1 point per pair played)

Crab, Boat, Fish, Swimmer, Shark — each pair played is worth 1 point.

### Multiplier Cards

Boat, Fish, Penguin, and Captain (Sailor) multipliers each add 1 bonus point per matching card you have.

### Mermaid Cards

There are 4 Mermaid cards. If any player collects all 4, they **win the game immediately**.

## End of Round

Add round scores to cumulative totals. If no player has reached the target score, start a new round. The player to the left of the one who ended the previous round goes first.

**Special case**: If the draw pile runs out during a player's turn, the round ends immediately with **no scoring**.

## Winning

The first player to reach or exceed the **target score** wins. If multiple players cross the threshold in the same round, the player with the higher score wins.
