# GoTown (Skyscraper) Rules

## Overview

GoTown is a competitive card game published by Helvetiq, designed by Morten & Alexander Bonavent. Each player races to build a five-story skyscraper first. Floors are built by combining two cards that sum to 9. But watch out — opponents can steal your floors, destroy them, or mess with your plans!

## Components

- 32 Floor cards (values 1-8, 4 of each)
- 4 Joker cards (wild — player chooses value when played)
- 18 Special cards:
  - Thief / Super Thief
  - Jackhammer / Wrecking Ball
  - Guard Dog / Bone
  - Food Truck (skip turn)

## Setup

1. Shuffle all cards together.
2. Deal **5 cards** to each player.
3. Place the remaining deck face down as the draw pile.
4. Choose a starting player. Play proceeds clockwise.

## How to Play

On your turn:

1. **Draw 1 card** from the draw pile.
2. **Perform any number of actions** (in any order):

### Build a Floor
Play **2 floor cards** from your hand that add up to exactly **9** (e.g., 3+6, 4+5, 1+8). Place them side by side in front of you as one floor of your tower. Stack new floors on top of previous ones.

**Joker cards** are wild — they can represent any value when building.

### Steal a Floor
Play **1 floor card** that complements a solitary card on an opponent's top floor (the two must sum to 9). Take their card and combine it with yours to build a floor on your own tower.

- Jokers on opponents' floors can be stolen, but they keep their assigned value.
- Jokers in your hand **cannot** be used to steal.

### Play Special Cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Guard Dog** | Attach to any of your floors. Protects it from being stolen or destroyed. |
| **Bone** | Removes a Guard Dog from any floor (yours or opponent's). |
| **Jackhammer** | Destroy an opponent's **top floor** (unprotected). Both cards go to discard. |
| **Wrecking Ball** | Destroy **any floor** of an opponent's tower (unprotected), not just the top. |
| **Thief** | Steal 1 random card from an opponent's hand. |
| **Super Thief** | Look at an opponent's hand and steal 1 card of your choice. |
| **Food Truck** | Force an opponent to **skip their next turn**. |

## Key Rules

- There is no hand limit.
- If the draw pile runs out, shuffle the discard pile to form a new draw pile.
- A floor protected by a Guard Dog cannot be stolen or destroyed until the Dog is removed with a Bone.
- You can build multiple floors in a single turn if you have the cards.

## Winning

The **first player** to complete **5 floors** on their tower wins immediately!
