# UNO Flip Rules

## Overview

UNO Flip is a twist on classic UNO featuring a double-sided deck. The deck has a **Light Side** (gentle, similar to classic UNO) and a **Dark Side** (harsh, with more punitive action cards). Players must flip the deck between sides during play and be the first to empty their hand.

## Components

- 112 double-sided cards (Light Side / Dark Side)
- Each side has its own set of action cards

## Setup

1. Choose a dealer. Shuffle the deck with the Light Side facing up.
2. Deal 7 cards to each player, Light Side up.
3. Place the remaining deck in the center as the draw pile.
4. Flip the top card to start the discard pile. If it's an action card, follow its effect.

## Light Side Cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Draw One** (Blue/Green/Yellow/Red) | Next player draws 1 card and skips their turn |
| **Reverse** | Reverses direction of play |
| **Skip** | Next player loses their turn |
| **Flip** | All players flip the deck AND their hands to the Dark Side |
| **Wild** | Player chooses the next color |
| **Wild Draw Two** | Player chooses color; next player draws 2 and loses turn |

## Dark Side Cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Draw Five** | Next player draws 5 cards and skips their turn |
| **Reverse** | Reverses direction of play |
| **Skip Everyone** | All other players lose their turn |
| **Flip** | All players flip the deck AND their hands back to the Light Side |
| **Wild** | Player chooses the next color |
| **Wild Draw Color** | Next player draws until they get the chosen color, then loses turn |

## How to Play

1. On your turn, play a card matching the top of the discard pile by **color**, **number**, or **symbol**.
2. If you cannot play, draw one card. You may play it immediately if it matches.
3. When playing your second-to-last card, say **"UNO!"** before your next turn begins. If caught not saying it, draw 2 penalty cards.
4. The Flip card switches the entire game between Light Side and Dark Side. When flipped, all cards in hand and the draw/discard piles are turned over.

## Winning

The first player to empty their hand wins the round. Points are scored from opponents' remaining cards:

| Card Type | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Number cards | Face value |
| Draw One / Draw Five | 10 |
| Reverse / Skip / Skip Everyone / Flip | 20 |
| Wild | 40 |
| Wild Draw Two / Wild Draw Color | 50 |

First to 500 points wins the game.
