# UNO Show 'Em No Mercy Rules

## Overview

UNO Show 'Em No Mercy is the most brutal version of UNO, published by Mattel. With 168 cards, massive draw penalties, stacking rules, hand-swapping mechanics, and the merciless elimination rule, there are two ways to win: empty your hand or knock everyone else out.

## Setup

1. Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
2. Deal **7 cards** to each player.
3. Place remaining cards face down as the draw pile.
4. Flip the top card to start the discard pile.

## How to Play

Match the top discard by **color**, **number**, or **symbol**. If you can't play, draw one card (play it immediately if it matches). When you're down to 1 card, yell **"UNO!"** — if caught, draw **4 penalty cards**.

## Special Rules

### Stacking Rule
Draw cards **CAN be stacked**. If someone plays a Draw card on you (+2, +4, +6, +10), you may play a Draw card of **equal or higher value** to pass the penalty to the next player. The total accumulates until a player cannot stack, and they must draw **all** accumulated cards.

### 7's Swap
When you play a **7** (any color), you **must** swap your entire hand with another player of your choice.

### 0's Pass
When you play a **0** (any color), **all players** must pass their entire hand to the next player in the current direction of play.

### Mercy Rule (Elimination)
If a player ever has **25 or more cards** in their hand, they are **immediately eliminated** from the game. Their cards are set aside for reshuffling when the draw pile runs out.

## Action Cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Skip** | Next player loses their turn |
| **Reverse** | Reverses direction of play |
| **Draw Two (+2)** | Next player draws 2 and loses their turn. Can be stacked. |
| **Draw Four (+4)** | Next player draws 4 and loses their turn. Can be stacked. |
| **Discard All** | Discard ALL cards from your hand matching this card's color |
| **Skip Everyone** | All other players skip their turn. You play again immediately. |
| **Wild Color Roulette** | Choose a color. Next player draws cards from the pile until they reveal the chosen color. |
| **Wild Reverse Draw Four** | Reverses direction AND the new next player draws 4 |
| **Wild Draw Six** | Choose a color. Next player draws 6 and loses their turn. |
| **Wild Draw Ten** | Choose a color. Next player draws 10 and loses their turn. |

## Winning

Two ways to win:
1. **Empty your hand** — be the first to play all your cards
2. **Last player standing** — all other players eliminated by the Mercy Rule
