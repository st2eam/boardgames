# Wind Sound Again (Attack by Stratagem) Rules

## Overview

Wind Sound Again (风声再临) is a spy-themed hidden identity card game for 3-7 players, inspired by the acclaimed Chinese film "The Message" (风声). Players are divided into three factions — Latent Agents, Military Intelligence, and Agents — and must pass intelligence cards to allies while deducing the identities of enemies. Cards serve a dual purpose: as intelligence (by color) and as action cards (by text effect).

## Components

- 8 Identity cards (faction assignments)
- 25 Character cards (each with unique abilities)
- 87 Base cards (dual-use: intelligence + action)
- 1 Rulebook

## Factions & Victory Conditions

| Faction | Color | Victory Condition |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| **Latent (潜伏)** | Red | Any teammate collects 3 red intelligence cards |
| **Military Intelligence (军情)** | Blue | Any teammate collects 3 blue intelligence cards |
| **Agent (特工)** | Solo | Complete your secret mission OR collect 6 true intelligence (red or blue) |

**Death**: Any player who collects 3 **black** intelligence cards dies immediately.

## Setup

1. Select identity cards based on player count and deal one face-down to each player. Keep your identity secret.
2. Each player draws 3 character cards and secretly chooses 1 to play. Return unused characters to the box.
3. All players reveal their chosen characters simultaneously. Some characters may be placed face-down (with hidden abilities triggered when flipped).
4. Shuffle the base cards and deal 2 cards to each player as starting hand. Hand limit is 6 cards.

## Understanding the Cards

Each base card has **dual use**:
- **As Intelligence**: The card's background color (red / blue / black) and transmission type (top-right icon) determine its intelligence properties
- **As Action Card**: The text at the bottom describes a functional effect when played during the action phase

### Intelligence Transmission Types

| Type | Method |
|------|--------|
| **Secret Telegram (密电)** | Passed face-down clockwise. Each player may accept or decline. |
| **Document (文本)** | Passed face-up clockwise. Each player may accept or decline. |
| **Direct Delivery (直达)** | Sent face-down directly to one chosen player. They may accept or decline. |

If all players decline, the intelligence returns to the sender who **must** accept it.

## Turn Structure

Each turn follows these phases in order:

### 1. Draw Phase
Draw 2 cards from the draw pile.

### 2. Action Phase
You may play action cards from your hand (no limit on number). Trigger the text effect on each card played.

### 3. Intelligence Phase (Mandatory)
You **must** send exactly 1 card from your hand as intelligence. Choose a card and send it according to its transmission type. The receiving player places it face-up in front of them (ignoring text effect, only the color matters).

### 4. Discard Phase
Discard down to the hand limit of 6 cards.

## Character Abilities

Each character has a unique ability printed on their card. Some are always active, some trigger under specific conditions, and some hidden characters activate a one-time powerful effect when revealed.

## Winning

The game ends **immediately** when any faction's victory condition is met. That faction's members win together (or the Agent wins alone).
