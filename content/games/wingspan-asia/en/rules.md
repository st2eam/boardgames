# Wingspan: Asia Rules

## Overview

Wingspan Asia is the third expansion for Wingspan, adding 90 new Asian bird cards, a standalone Duet mode for 1–2 players, and Flock mode for 6–7 players. It can be played as a card expansion mixed with the base game, or as a standalone game using the Duet mode. For Flock mode (6-7 players), the base game is required for additional player components.

## Components

- 90 bird cards (Asian birds)
- 14 bonus cards
- 2 double-sided player mats (standard & Oceania)
- 16 action cubes
- 1 duet map board
- 6 duet end-round goal tiles
- 30 duet tokens
- 1 turn-order dial (Flock mode)
- 1 round-end goal board (Flock mode)
- 2 swift-start guides
- 81 food tokens
- 30 wooden egg tokens
- 5 food dice
- Birdfeeder board and bird tray
- First-player token and scorepad
- 18 Automa cards

## Setup (as Card Expansion)

Shuffle the 90 Asian bird cards and 14 bonus cards into the corresponding base game decks. All bird powers are fully compatible with the base game and all previous expansions.

## New Game Modes

### Duet Mode (1–2 Players)

Duet mode uses a dedicated **Duet Map** board instead of standard goal tiles. It can be played standalone or integrated with any card mix.

**Setup:**
- Give each player 15 duet tokens in their color.
- Place the Duet Map in the center of the table.
- Use the 6 duet-specific goal tiles.

**Duet Token Placement:**
Whenever you play a bird card, you place a duet token on the Duet Map in a space matching:
1. The bird's **habitat** type (Forest, Grassland, or Wetlands), AND
2. One of the bird's **traits** — either its nest type, food cost (number of food icons), or wingspan length.

**Scoring:**
- Each space on the duet map can hold at most **1 token per player**.
- **Connected groups** of your duet tokens score points at the end of the game. The larger the contiguous group, the more points.
- The **reset box** on the map can hold an unlimited number of tokens from either player.
- Duet map bonuses: When placing a token on a bonus space, gain the shown bonus (food die or card draw).

### Flock Mode (6–7 Players)

Flock mode allows up to 7 players by splitting into two groups:

- Players are divided into **two groups**, each sharing a birdfeeder and bird card deck.
- **Two players take turns simultaneously** (one from each group) to speed up gameplay.
- A **turn-order dial** tracks which players in each group are active.
- Uses a dedicated **Flock round-end goal board**.
- Requires the base game for additional player components (mats, cubes, eggs).

## Notable Rules

- **Great Cormorant**: Skip the first clause (moving a fish) for its first activation — just roll 2 dice. Once a fish is cached, the full ability activates on subsequent turns.
- **Nectar caching**: Nectar can be cached as [wild] food. Cached nectar stays on the bird and is not placed in "spent nectar" — it's not discarded at round's end.
- **Anatomist bonus card**: If using both the original and Asia versions, use the Asia version's updated point values.
- **Mixing duet and standard goals**: Unofficially, you can mix Duet and standard end-round goals, but this de-emphasizes duet map tokens.
