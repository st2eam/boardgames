# Wingspan: European Expansion Rules

## Overview

The European Expansion is the first expansion for Wingspan, adding 81 new bird cards featuring birds from Europe, new round-end bird powers (teal), sideways birds, and additional goal tiles and bonus cards. This expansion requires the base game to play.

## Components

- 81 bird cards (European birds)
- 15 purple egg miniatures
- 5 bonus cards
- 4 Automa cards
- 5 goal tiles
- 38 food tokens
- 1 reference tile
- 1 scorepad
- 1 custom storage tray

## Setup

Shuffle the expansion bird cards, bonus cards, and goal tiles into the corresponding base game decks. Mix in the extra eggs and food tokens. Place the new reference tile near the goal mat — the **green side** of the goal mat is recommended, as some new goals don't work well with the blue side.

## New Mechanics

### Round End Powers (Teal)

The biggest new addition. Round-end bird powers resolve after all turns in the round are complete, but **before** scoring the end-of-round goal:

- Resolve in player order, starting with the first player of that round.
- If you have multiple round-end birds, you may resolve them in any order.
- Round end powers do **NOT** trigger "Once Between Turns" (pink) powers.

### Sideways / Overlapping Birds

Some birds (e.g., **European Roller**) are played **sideways**, covering two adjacent spaces in the same habitat row. Others (e.g., **Common Buzzard**) can be played on top of a sideways bird, also placed sideways and completely covering it.

- You must still match the habitat when playing these birds.
- The two covered spaces must both be in the same habitat row.
- A sideways bird has two brown "when activated" triggers — one for each covered column.

### "Count Double" Birds

Some birds count double toward end-of-round goals — but only for goals that count **birds** (e.g., "birds with platform nests"), not goals counting eggs, food costs, or other quantities.

### Alternative Costs (Marked with ✱)

Birds with an asterisk (✱) in their food cost have an alternative cost explained in the card text (e.g., hunting/fishing powers where you roll dice outside the birdfeeder).

## New End-of-Round Goals

The expansion adds 5 new goal tiles:
- Food in personal supply
- Birds worth over 4 points
- Birds with no eggs
- Birds in one row (most populated habitat)
- Filled columns (all 3 spaces in a column)
- Brown powers / White & no powers
- Birds with tucked cards
- Food cost of played birds

## FAQ

- **Can I play the expansion without the base game?** No — it requires components from the core game.
- **Do a sideways bird's two brown powers both activate?** Yes. When you activate that habitat, both activate (right to left across all cards, including each column of a sideways bird).
- **Bird tray refill with sideways birds?** Sideways birds count as one card but occupy two tray slots visually — use common sense placement.
