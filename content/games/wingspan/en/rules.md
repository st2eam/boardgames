# Wingspan Rules

## Overview

Wingspan is a competitive, medium-weight, card-driven engine-building board game for 1–5 players. You play as bird enthusiasts seeking to attract the best birds to your wildlife preserves. The winner is whoever has the most points after 4 rounds.

Each bird you play extends a chain of powerful combinations in one of three habitats: **Forest**, **Grassland**, and **Wetlands**. These habitats focus on key gameplay elements: gaining food tokens, laying eggs, and drawing bird cards.

## Components

- 170 bird cards
- 26 bonus cards
- 1 bird tray
- 5 player mats
- 40 action cubes (8 per player)
- 103 food tokens (fruit, worm, seed, fish, rodent)
- 75 egg miniatures
- 5 food dice
- 1 birdfeeder dice tower
- 8 goal tiles
- 1 first-player token
- 1 scorepad

## Setup

1. Shuffle all 170 bird cards and place them in the bird tray, filling all 3 face-up slots.
2. Shuffle the green-backed bonus cards into a face-down deck.
3. Shuffle the 8 goal tiles and place one (random side up) on each of the 4 round spaces on the goal board.
4. Place all food tokens and egg miniatures in a shared supply.
5. Roll all 5 food dice into the birdfeeder dice tower.
6. Each player receives:
   - 1 player mat (Forest / Grassland / Wetlands)
   - 8 action cubes in their color
   - 5 bird cards (drawn randomly, kept secret)
   - 1 of each food token (5 total: fruit, worm, seed, fish, rodent)
   - 2 bonus cards (keep 1, discard the other)
7. Choose any number of bird cards to keep from the 5 drawn. For each card kept, discard 1 food token from your starting supply. Return discarded cards to the deck and reshuffle.
8. Randomly choose a starting player and give them the first-player token.

## How to Play

The game lasts **4 rounds**, with turns decreasing each round:

| Round | Turns per Player |
|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | 8 |
| 2 | 7 |
| 3 | 6 |
| 4 | 5 |

On your turn, you may do **one** of four actions:

### 1. Play a Bird from Your Hand

Select one bird card from your hand. To play it:

- **Pay the food cost** shown in the top-left of the card. A wild symbol (gray coin) means any food type. You may substitute **any 2 food tokens for 1 food** of any type.
- **Pay the egg cost** based on the column you place the bird in. Column 1 = 0 eggs, columns 2–3 = 1 egg, columns 4–5 = 2 eggs. Eggs must come from birds already on your mat.
- Place the bird in a **matching habitat** row (Forest, Grassland, or Wetlands) based on the icon at the top-left.
- If the bird has a **"When Played" (white)** power, activate it now.

### 2. Gain Food (Forest Habitat)

- Place an action cube on the leftmost exposed slot in your Forest row.
- Gain food tokens equal to the number of dice icons shown at your cube's position.
- Choose dice from the birdfeeder and take matching tokens. A die showing two options (e.g., worm/grain) lets you choose either.
- If your Forest slot shows a **card-for-food** icon, you may discard 1 bird card to gain 1 additional die/food.
- After taking food, re-roll all 5 dice **only when** the birdfeeder is empty **or** all remaining dice show the same face.
- Activate any **brown "When Activated"** powers on Forest birds from **right to left**.

### 3. Lay Eggs (Grassland Habitat)

- Place an action cube on the leftmost exposed slot in your Grassland row.
- Gain eggs equal to the number of egg icons shown at your cube's position.
- Place eggs on your bird cards. Each bird has a maximum egg limit (shown as nest icon with number). Excess eggs are lost.
- If your Grassland slot shows a **food-for-egg** icon, you may pay 1 food to gain 1 extra egg.
- Activate any **brown "When Activated"** powers on Grassland birds from **right to left**.

### 4. Draw Bird Cards (Wetlands Habitat)

- Place an action cube on the leftmost exposed slot in your Wetlands row.
- Draw cards equal to the number of card icons shown at your cube's position.
- Draw from the 3 face-up cards in the tray **or** from the top of the deck (or mix and match).
- Do not refill the bird tray until the end of your turn.
- If your Wetlands slot shows an **egg-for-card** icon, you may discard 1 egg to draw 1 extra card.
- Activate any **brown "When Activated"** powers on Wetlands birds from **right to left**.

## Bird Card Powers

| Color | Trigger | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **White** | When Played | Activates once, when you play the bird |
| **Brown** | When Activated | Activates each time you take that habitat's action |
| **Pink** | Once Between Turns | Activates on other players' turns under specific conditions |

When activating brown powers in a habitat, always go **right to left** — the most recently played bird activates last.

## Round End

After all players have placed all their action cubes for the round:

1. Activate any **"Round End"** bird powers (from expansions).
2. **Score the round goal**: Each player places 1 action cube on the goal board based on how they performed relative to other players. First place scores 5 points, second place 3, third place 1 (in a 3+ player game).
3. Remove all action cubes from player mats.
4. Discard all face-up bird cards from the tray and refill with 3 new cards.
5. Pass the first-player token clockwise.
6. Begin the next round.

## Game End and Scoring

After Round 4 ends and the final goal is scored, tally points:

| Source | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Bird point values | Printed on each bird card (feather icons on left) |
| Eggs | 1 point per egg on your birds |
| Cached food | 1 point per food token cached on bird cards |
| Tucked cards | 1 point per card tucked under other birds |
| Bonus cards | Points from your kept bonus card objectives |
| End-of-round goals | Total points from all 4 rounds |

**Highest total wins!** Tiebreaker: most unused food tokens. Second tiebreaker: shared victory.

## Key Concepts

- **No hand limit**: You can hold as many bird cards as you like.
- **No food limit**: No cap on food tokens you can stockpile.
- **Caching**: Some bird powers let you place food tokens on cards for end-game points.
- **Tucking/Flocking**: Some powers let you tuck cards under other birds for points.
- **Star/Wild nests** (⭐): Count as any nest type for round goals and bonus cards.
- **Two food = one wild**: When paying bird costs, any 2 food tokens can substitute for 1 required food type.
- **Egg limits**: Each bird card shows its maximum egg capacity.
